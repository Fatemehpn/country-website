Tehran, the capital city, was a vibrant mix of modernity and tradition. The Grand Bazaar was a shopper's paradise, filled with everything from spices to beautiful Persian carpets. One of the highlights of my trip was visiting Isfahan with its stunning mosques and picturesque bridges. The Imam Square is absolutely breathtaking at sunset. My trip to Iran was an eye-opener and truly one of the best travel experiences I’ve ever had. From the ancient ruins of Persepolis to the charming city of Shiraz, every part of Iran has its own unique charm. The Persian cuisine is a treat for the senses – don’t miss out on trying kebabs and saffron rice. Yazd, with its desert architecture and historic windcatchers, was a highlight. The local people are some of the friendliest I’ve ever met, always eager to share their culture and stories. Iran is a hidden gem that more travelers should discover. It’s safe, stunning, and steeped in history.
